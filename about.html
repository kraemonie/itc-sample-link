We Make the World Blush.

Welcome to Blush Babe. We are a homegrown beauty brand that champions inclusivity, self-expression, and the Filipino spirit. Born out of a desire to create products that work for our unique climate and skin tones, we have grown into a community of beauty lovers.


Our mission is simple: Beauty in every shade. Whether you are looking for that perfect everyday tint or a bold statement lip, we have something for you. Our products are cruelty-free, multi-use, and designed for real life.
